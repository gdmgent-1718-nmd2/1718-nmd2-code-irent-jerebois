We're a small team of engineers, trying to reinvent existing object and 'upgrading' them to the 21st century.

One day Joe walked into our lab with a balloon, but not just any balloon, this balloon looked different. It was almost unprickable, a science experiment by MIT into creating very flexible but durable rubber.

One of the tests with helium ended up stretching a part of the experimental material that was for the test made to resemble a balloon. This gave Joe an idea and brought the inflated experiment into our offices.

3

4

We were thinking about new exciting ideas for social application, then came this balloon.

After many tests we noticed that the balloon was able to light up with an electric current, different currents would result in different color, electroluminescense (in a nutshell).

5

6

We ran a succesfull Kickstarter campaign, and now here we are, renting world's first 'smart' balloon!

Our future goals are to be able to sell the balloon, but as it stands vast mass-production at low pricing is still a way off.
